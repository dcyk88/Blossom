Blossom Community Connect

A Community Intelligence Platform for Active Ageing

Blossom Community Connect is a digital enabler supporting relationship-based outreach and community integration for the proposed Active Ageing Centre at Blk 210, Yishun Street 21.

It maps the seniors’ ecosystem across Yishun Central & Northland — eldercare, healthcare, daily-living touchpoints, places of worship, green spaces and community nodes — to guide outreach, partnerships and programming under Blossom Seeds (福善).
